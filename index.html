About


The Dublin Coders Community is a community of Middle schoolers around Dublin, Ohio who are bored over the summmer and want to create and play with code. We are planning to make challenges and compettitions and maybe learn some new things! The group was made by Rudy but it can have any one in it! We are open to anyone from Dublin. We originally started as a coding server just for Karrer but now we have expanded to the whole Dublin. That way, we provide a welcoming experince to learn code and to play with it.
